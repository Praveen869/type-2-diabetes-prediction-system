The Model

Machine Learning at its Core

Our prediction engine is trained on the famous Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset, originally from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases.

The dataset contains diagnostic measurements from 768 female patients of Pima Indian heritage, aged 21 and over. The model learns patterns from 8 key clinical features to classify diabetes risk.
